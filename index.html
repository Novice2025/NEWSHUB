Featured Story: Global Developments

Former US President Donald Trump has strongly criticized news organizations like CNN and The New York Times. He claims their reports on a recent intelligence assessment about US strikes on Iran are "fake news." The assessment reportedly suggests the strikes only set back Iran's nuclear program by a few months, not "obliterated" it as Trump claimed. This highlights ongoing tension between Trump and certain media outlets.

Click for Vocabulary & Translation

Key Vocabulary (English):

  • Criticized (verb): Expressed disapproval of someone or something.
  • Intelligence Assessment: An official evaluation of secret information.
  • Set back (phrasal verb): Delayed the progress of.
  • Obliterated: Completely destroyed.
  • Tension: A state of mental or emotional strain; strained relations between groups.

Vocabulário Chave (Português):

  • Criticized (verb): **Criticou** (Expressou desaprovação de alguém ou algo.)
  • Intelligence Assessment: **Avaliação de Inteligência** (Uma avaliação oficial de informações secretas.)
  • Set back (phrasal verb) = Atrasou: Delayed the progress of.
  • Obliterated: **Exterminou/Destruiu completamente** (Destruiu completamente.)
  • Tension: **Tensão** (Um estado de estresse mental ou emocional; relações tensas entre grupos.)

Grammar Focus: Reporting Verbs

Notice "He **claims** their reports..." and "The assessment **reportedly** suggests..." These words introduce information from another source without stating it as a fact.

**Discuss & Practice:** How do you think political leaders and the media should communicate about important national events?
